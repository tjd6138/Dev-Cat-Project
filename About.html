Why We Do What We Do

Come join Chester County Bands where you will be surrounded by dedicated faculty and passionate students just like you. Here at Chester County, our faculty gladly devotes their profession to your journey to success in education. Faculty ensures to provide you with high quality education in music that will supply you with a set of skills, such as discpline, attentiveness, and social skills, to later help you and other students in careers. As you partcipate in our band program, you will learn to understand your musicality as an artist. Our ensembles encourage an open community to enhance your musical abilities while having access to facutly for further help. When in Chester County Band, you're sure to recieve an experience of artistic enrichment that will loom beyond high school.


Faculty

Jennifer Cupples

Band Director

Mrs. Cupples is originally from Gibson County and graduated in 1999 with a Bachelors in music education from the University of Tennessee at Martin. Mrs. Cupples has taught in Jackson-Madison County Schools for 16 years and is currently the Director of Bands in the Chester County School System. She teaches 6th grade Beginning Band, 7th and 8th grade Intermediate Band, 9th - 12th grade High School Concert, Marching and Jazz Bands, Color Guard Class, and Music History Classes. In addition to teaching, Mrs. Cupples plays French horn in the orchestra at Englewood Baptist Church in Jackson, TN. She currently lives in Henderson, Tennessee with her husband, Jeff Cupples, and two children, Jaxon and Jacie.

Josh Gatlin

Assistant Band Director

Mr. Gatlin is in the midst of his fourth year teaching and second year teaching 6th - 12th Vocal music and assisting the 6th - 12th grade Band at Chester County.
